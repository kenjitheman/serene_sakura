# Serene Sakura - Anime Recommendation Engine

Serene Sakura is an anime recommendation engine written in Rust, designed to provide users with personalized anime recommendations based on their preferences. Whether you're a seasoned anime enthusiast or a newcomer to the world of anime, Serene Sakura aims to enhance your viewing experience by suggesting anime titles that align with your tastes.

## Features

- **Personalized Recommendations**: Serene Sakura analyzes your viewing history and preferences to offer tailored anime recommendations.

- **Fast and Efficient**: Built with Rust, Serene Sakura prioritizes performance, ensuring quick and efficient recommendations even with large datasets.
